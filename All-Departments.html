St.Francis Departments

All Departments

SFIT offers four-year Bachelor’s programs in three branches of engineering, namely,
  • Computer Engineering with an intake of 120 students
  • Information Technology with an intake of 120 students
  • Electronics and Telecommunications with an intake of 120 students
The Science & HSS (first year) department comprises of a team of faculty from applied mathematics, applied science, engineering mechanics and drawing and humanities.

Each of these departments have well equipped laboratories, classrooms and tutorial rooms with modern teaching aids, computing facility and departmental library.
