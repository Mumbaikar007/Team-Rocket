Bio

With an objective of producing and nurturing the future budding entrepreneurs and early researchers, Computer Engineering department was established in 1999. The undergraduate course in Computer Engineering was started in same year with an intake of 60 seats which now has been reached up to 120 since 2010. The postgraduate course has also been introduced in year 2012 with the intake of 18. The department is a recognized research center which is affiliated to university of Mumbai for its Ph.D. program. Each year about 25% of the final degree year students secure admission for higher studies at top and eminent Universities across the globe and reputed institutes in India. And as a result of this many of our students are emerging as Entrepreneurs. Total of 90% of our eligible students are placed in reputed organization every year. The competent faculties, dynamic students, specialized laboratories are the strengths of our department. We have our own E-cell platform for motivating students for entrepreneurship and self-development. Every year team of young engineers exhibit their advanced and technical skills in ‘Robocon’ and ‘e-yantra’ competition. We have experienced and highly competent faculty members for all core and emerging courses of computer engineering such as Advance Data base management systems, Data structure and algorithms, Web technology, Soft Computing, ERP, Image processing, Big Data, Digital forensics, cloud computing and computer security.

Department of Computer Engineering has enthusiastic team of staff and students for co-ordination and execution of the Computer Society of India (professional Body) activities throughout the year. Being Heart of Our Institute SFIT, we are giving strength, motivation, ethical values to our young aspiring engineers besides technical and professional skill sets. We always encourage our students to be socially aware, responsible, committed, and to become smart citizens of today’s era.

In line with our vision, we are constantly striving hard to improve in quality and remain committed to focus on excellence at par with global standards.

I welcome you to join our endeavors to deliver and bring out the best for students at SFIT as a competitive edge.
