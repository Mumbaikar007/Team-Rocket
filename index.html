Welcome


St. Francis Institute of Technology (SFIT) was established in 1999 with a view to create an Engineering Degree Institute to fulfill the long felt public need of an Institute of Excellence in Borivli.

SFIT has three UG Programs and two PG Programs in the most coveted disciplines considering both industrial need and public preference. These courses would lead to award of the Degree of Bachelor of Engineering (B. E.) and Master of Engineering (M.E.) and PhD in related discipline of Engineering.

SFIT is approved by AICTE and is permanently affiliated to the University of Mumbai.

National Board of Accreditation (NBA) has granted accreditation to all the three U.G. programs offered by our Institute. The institute is also certified for meeting with the Quality Management System Standards ISO 9001:2008, for providing undergraduate courses in Engineering/Technology.

Why SFIT

Vision

"To be a chrysalis where bright youngsters are transformed into technological entrepreneurs and innovative leaders of tomorrow’s world, consistent with the Franciscan vision of integrity, peace and love."

Quality policy

"To be the most preferred Institute of Engineering and Technology equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, to develop technically qualified techno-entrepreneurial class of value based global industry leaders, imparting comprehensive technical education, through highly competent and dedicated staff, ensuring continual improvement of QMS processes, meeting ISO 9001-2008 statutory and regulatory requirements."
